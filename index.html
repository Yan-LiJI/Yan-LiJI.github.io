About me

Yanli Ji is currently a Professor at Sun Yat-sen University (Shenzhen). She obtained her Ph.D degree from the Department of Advanced Information Technology, Kyushu University, Japan in Sep. 2012. She was a visiting researcher at the University of Tokyo from Nov. 2018 to Jan. 2020. Her research interests focus on Human-Centered Robot topics, e.g. human activities, emotions, embodied intelligence, etc.

教授，博导，博士毕业于日本九州大学，东京大学客员研究员。入选深圳市高层次人才孔雀计划，入选2023年度AI华人女性青年学者榜。发表中科院JCR二区以上SCI期刊及CCF A国际会议论文50余篇，申请三十余项发明专利。中国计算机学会高级会员(CCF Senior Member)，中国图象图形学会青年工作委员会执行委员、副秘书长，VALSE 委员会 SAC主席。参与组织包括ACMMM 2021，ACMMM Asia 2021，ACCV2022等国际会议5次，国内学术峰会视觉与学习青年学者研讨会（VALSE）及 ACM SIGAI CHINA symposium in TURC等15余次。28th Australasian Database Conference国际会议Best Paper Award；第二十届中国虚拟现实大会最佳论文提名奖。
